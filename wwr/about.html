Our Mission

Purpose: To offer thrilling water rafting experiences that blend excitement, nature appreciation, and teamwork.

History

Founded in 2010 by avid outdoors enthusiasts Alex Turner and Sarah Mitchell, Rapids Adventure Co. emerged as a beacon of thrilling water rafting experiences nestled in scenic landscapes. With a commitment to safety, environmental stewardship, and fostering camaraderie, the company swiftly gained acclaim in the adventure tourism industry. Over the past decade, Rapids Adventure Co. has evolved into a trusted name, offering not only exhilarating rafting journeys but also team-building events and educational programs. A symbol of adventure and exploration, the company remains dedicated to creating unforgettable moments that inspire a profound connection with nature and the joy of conquering the rapids.
